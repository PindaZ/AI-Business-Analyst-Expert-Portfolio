# Lab: Mapping the AI Adoption Curve

## 🎯 Objective
As a BA, you are a "Bridge" between the tech and the people. In this lab, you will map the current state of "AI Adoption" within a mock department, "Claims Processing," and design a change management strategy.

## 🏢 Scenario Context
**Department:** Claims Processing (50 employees).
**Problem:** A new AI agent is being introduced to auto-generate draft claim letters. 
*   **The "Innovators" (5 people):** Are already using their own ChatGPT accounts to write letters.
*   **The "Early Majority" (20 people):** Are curious but afraid of making a mistake.
*   **The "Late Majority" (15 people):** Are skeptical and think the AI is "cold" and "robotic."
*   **The "Laggards" (10 people):** Are convinced the AI is a precursor to layoffs and are actively resisting the rollout.

---

## 📝 Exercise 1: Segmenting the Workforce
Define a specific communication strategy for each of the 4 segments above. 

*   **For the Innovators:** (e.g., Make them "AI Champions" to mentor others).
*   **For the Laggards:** (e.g., Focus on "Job Enhancement" rather than "Automation").

---

## 📊 Exercise 2: The "AI-Expert BA" Workshop
Design a 1-hour workshop for the "Late Majority" to demonstrate the AI's value. 
**Task:** List 3 specific "Live Demo" activities you would show them to prove the AI is their *assistant*, not their *replacement*.

---

## ✅ Deliverable
Draft a 1-page "Change Management Roadmap" (Timeline: Month 1-3).
*   **Month 1:** Pilot Group Launch.
*   **Month 2:** Feedback Loops & Fine-Tuning.
*   **Month 3:** Full Deployment & KPI Measurement.
