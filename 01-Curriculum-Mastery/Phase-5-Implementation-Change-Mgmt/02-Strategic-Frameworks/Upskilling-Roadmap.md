# AI-Upskilling-Roadmap

The AI-Upskilling-Roadmap is a strategic framework for ensuring employees are equipped with the skills needed to use and develop AI systems effectively.

## The Three Levels of AI Upskilling

1.  **Foundational (Level 1)**:
    *   **Goal**: Understanding what AI is and how it can be used at a basic level.
    *   **Focus**: AI literacy, basic prompting, and ethical considerations.
2.  **Practitioner (Level 2)**:
    *   **Goal**: Developing the skills needed to use and customize AI tools in a business context.
    *   **Focus**: Advanced prompting, data analysis for AI, and AI-driven process optimization.
3.  **Expert (Level 3)**:
    *   **Goal**: Building and managing AI systems from a technical or strategic perspective.
    *   **Focus**: AI model selection, fine-tuning, RAG architecture, and AI governance.

## The Three Domains of AI Expertise

### 1. Business & Strategic
*   **Skill 1**: AI Opportunity Identification & Prioritization.
*   **Skill 2**: AI ROI & Success Measurement.
*   **Skill 3**: AI-Driven Change Management.

### 2. Technical & Data
*   **Skill 1**: Prompt Engineering & Iterative Design.
*   **Skill 2**: Data Quality & Data Readiness Assessment.
*   **Skill 3**: AI Integration & Solution Architecture.

### 3. Ethical & Governance
*   **Skill 1**: Responsible AI (RAI) Principles & Frameworks.
*   **Skill 2**: AI Policy Development & Compliance.
*   **Skill 3**: Algorithmic Bias Mitigation & Risk Management.

## The Upskilling Roadmap Lifecycle

- **Step 1: Skills Gap Analysis**: Identifying current vs. future AI skills needed.
- **Step 2: Training & Development**: Providing tailored AI training for different employee levels.
- **Step 3: Applied Learning**: Encouraging employees to use AI in their daily work.
- **Step 4: Continuous Learning**: Staying up-to-date with the latest AI advancements.
