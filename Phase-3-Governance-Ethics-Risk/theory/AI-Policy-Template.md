# AI-Policy-Template

The AI-Policy-Template provides a strategic framework for organizations to define and communicate their stance on AI usage and development.

## 1. Scope & Purpose

*   **Who does this apply to?**: Employees, contractors, third-party partners.
*   **What does it cover?**: LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT), predictive models, and custom AI solutions.
*   **Why is this policy needed?**: To ensure the ethical, legal, and secure use of AI across the enterprise.

## 2. Acceptable Use Policy

*   **Internal Data Use**: Employees must not input proprietary or customer-sensitive data into public-facing AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT).
*   **Output Validation**: All AI-generated content must be reviewed and validated by a human before being used for business decisions or external communication.
*   **Prohibited Uses**: E.g., using AI for discriminatory purposes, creating misinformation, or circumventing existing security controls.

## 3. Data Privacy & Security

*   **Data Minimization**: Only the minimum data necessary should be processed by AI systems.
*   **Model Ingestion**: Private data must only be used in enterprise-grade (e.g., Azure OpenAI) or on-premise AI environments.
*   **Access Controls**: Implementing role-based access control (RBAC) for all AI tools.

## 4. Governance & Accountability

*   **AI Oversight Committee**: Defining a central team responsible for approving AI use cases and reviewing compliance.
*   **Incident Reporting**: A clear process for reporting AI-related errors, bias, or security breaches.
*   **Auditability**: Maintaining logs of AI usage and model decisions for audit purposes.

## 5. Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Requirements

*   **Critical Decisions**: Requiring human review for high-risk decisions (e.g., hiring, lending, medical triage).
*   **Content Generation**: All external-facing AI content must include a disclaimer (e.g., "AI-Assisted Content").

## 6. Review & Enforcement

*   **Frequency**: This policy is reviewed quarterly to keep pace with AI advancements.
*   **Compliance**: Violations of this policy may lead to disciplinary action.
