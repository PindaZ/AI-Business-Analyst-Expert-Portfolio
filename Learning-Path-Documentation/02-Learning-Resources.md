# 02-Learning-Resources: Curated AI for Business Analysts

This curated list of high-impact resources will help you deepen your knowledge of the 60 advanced topics in the AI-Expert Business Analyst learning path.

## 📚 Essential Reading (Books)
- **"Competing in the Age of AI"** by Marco Iansiti and Karim R. Lakhani
- **"The Age of AI: And Our Human Future"** by Henry A. Kissinger, Eric Schmidt, and Daniel Huttenlocher
- **"Prediction Machines: The Simple Economics of Artificial Intelligence"** by Ajay Agrawal, Joshua Gans, and Avi Goldfarb
- **"AI 2041: Ten Visions for Our Future"** by Kai-Fu Lee and Chen Qiufan

## 🎓 High-Impact Courses (External)
- **[Google AI Essentials](https://grow.google/ai-essentials/)**: Foundational AI literacy and practical prompting.
- **[Azure AI Fundamentals (AI-900)](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/azure-ai-fundamentals/)**: AI/ML basics on the Microsoft Cloud.
- **[MIT: Artificial Intelligence: Implications for Business Strategy](https://executive.mit.edu/course/artificial-intelligence-implications-for-business-strategy/)**: High-level strategic alignment.
- **[Coursera: Generative AI for Everyone](https://www.coursera.org/learn/generative-ai-for-everyone)**: Andrew Ng's overview of GenAI for business users.
- **[Stanford: AI Graduate Certificate](https://online.stanford.edu/programs/artificial-intelligence-graduate-certificate)**: Technical training in TensorFlow and AI tools.

## 📄 Industry Whitepapers (Strategy & Value)
- **[McKinsey: The Economic Potential of Generative AI](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-digital/our-insights/the-economic-potential-of-generative-ai-the-next-productivity-frontier)**
- **[BCG: How Generative AI Will Transform Business Strategy](https://www.bcg.com/publications/2023/generative-ai-transform-business-strategy)**
- **[Gartner: 2024 Strategic Technology Trends - AI Trust, Risk, and Security Management (AI TRiSM)](https://www.gartner.com/en/articles/gartner-top-10-strategic-technology-trends-for-2024)**

## 🛠️ Specialized AI Tools for BAs
- **[Miro AI](https://miro.com/ai/)**: Generating sticky notes and diagrams from workshops.
- **[Lucidchart AI](https://www.lucidchart.com/pages/ai)**: Converting text descriptions into process flows.
- **[Otter.ai / Fireflies](https://otter.ai/)**: Capturing and transcribing meetings for requirement extraction.
- **[Notion AI](https://www.notion.so/product/ai)**: Summarizing notes and drafting requirement templates.

## 🔍 Continuous Learning (Newsletters & Blogs)
- **[The Batch (DeepLearning.AI)](https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/)**: Andrew Ng's weekly AI newsletter.
- **[Wait But Why: The AI Revolution](https://waitbutwhy.com/2015/01/artificial-intelligence-revolution-1.html)**: Tim Urban's deep dive into AI impact.
- **[Stratechery by Ben Thompson](https://stratechery.com/)**: Analysis of the business strategy behind tech.
