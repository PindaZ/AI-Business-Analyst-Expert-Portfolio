# 01-Curriculum-Map: The 6-Phase Master Curriculum

This curriculum map details the 60 advanced topics that make up the AI-Expert Business Analyst learning path. Each phase focuses on a specific critical domain in the enterprise AI lifecycle.

## 📊 Master Topics List

| Phase | Module | Focus Area |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1: Strategy** | **AI Strategy & Value Discovery** | Outcome-oriented AI, Agentic-First Strategy, Economics of Tokens, Value Chain Analysis, Sovereign AI, AI-First Innovation, ESG Goals, etc. |
| **2: Data** | **Data Strategy & Readiness** | Unstructured Data for Agents, Data Moats, Real-time Data Integration, Synthetic Data Generation, Data Lineage, Vector Databases, Semantic Search, Privacy-Preserving Strategies, etc. |
| **3: Governance** | **AI Governance, Ethics & Risk** | RAI Lifecycle, Agent Guardrails, AI Policy Template, EU AI Act Compliance, AI Accountability, Mitigating Hallucination Risk, Prompt Injection, Adversarial Attacks, Bias Auditing, HITL Design, etc. |
| **4: Architecture** | **Conceptual AI Architecture** | Agentic Workflow Blueprints, Multi-Agent Orchestration, Model Tiering, Router Architecture, SLMs vs LLMs, Agentic Memory, Multi-Modal Design, Scaling AI, Decentralized AI, LLM-as-a-Judge, etc. |
| **5: Implementation** | **Change Management** | AI Adoption Curve, Workflow Redesign for Collaboration, AI Headcount Upskilling, BA as AI Orchestrator, Transitioning from Chatbots to Agents, Retiring Legacy RPA, Employee Sentiment, Feedback Loops, etc. |
| **6: ROI** | **ROI & Success Measurement** | AI ROI Formula, Intangible Value Measurement, TCO for Multi-Agent Platforms, Financial Impact of Risk Mitigation, C-Suite Business Case, Agent Accuracy KPIs, Post-Implementation Audits, Market Share Gains, etc. |

## ⏳ Estimated Time per Phase (Self-Paced)

The curriculum is designed to be mastered over a 6-12 month period, depending on your prior experience:

- **Phase 1-2**: 4-6 weeks (Focus: Foundations and Strategic Frameworks)
- **Phase 3-4**: 8-12 weeks (Focus: Governance, Ethics, and Conceptual Architecture)
- **Phase 5-6**: 6-8 weeks (Focus: Implementation, Change Management, and ROI Measurement)

## 🎯 Outcomes
By following this map, you will be able to lead end-to-end AI initiatives, from initial value discovery to long-term success measurement, ensuring that AI is a strategic asset for your organization.
