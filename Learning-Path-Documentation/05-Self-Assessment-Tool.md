# 05-Self-Assessment-Tool: Comprehensive Diagnostic Rubric

Use this rigorous diagnostic tool to evaluate your current proficiency across the six core AI-BA domains. 
Score yourself from 1 to 5 for each domain based on your ability to execute the specific tasks listed.

## 📊 Proficiency Scale
- **1 (Novice)**: Awareness only; no practical experience.
- **2 (Beginner)**: Understands core concepts; can assist under supervision.
- **3 (Practitioner)**: Can execute tasks independently; uses tools effectively.
- **4 (Expert)**: Leads complex initiatives; designs custom frameworks; mentors others.
- **5 (Master)**: Strategically transforms organizations; develops industry-wide standards.

---

## 🧭 Assessment Rubric

### Domain 1: AI Strategy & Value Discovery
1. [ ] Can I conduct an AI Opportunity Prioritization Workshop using an objective matrix (e.g., Value vs. Complexity)?
2. [ ] Do I understand and can I articulate the shift from task-based RPA to outcome-oriented Agentic AI?
3. [ ] Can I analyze a company's value chain and identify where proprietary data creates a strategic AI moat?
4. [ ] Can I define clear, measurable business outcomes for a proposed AI initiative?
5. [ ] Do I understand the basic economics of GenAI (token costs, consumption models, build vs. buy)?

2. [ ] Do I understand the differences in preparing structured vs. unstructured data for Agentic consumption?
3. [ ] Can I articulate the strategic importance of a "Data Moat" to executive stakeholders?
4. [ ] Do I understand the fundamental concepts of Vector Databases and Semantic Search?
5. [ ] Can I identify data lineage requirements to ensure transparency in AI decision-making?

### Domain 3: AI Governance, Ethics & Risk
1. [ ] Can I design and implement Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) guardrails for autonomous agents?
2. [ ] Do I know how to audit a business process or dataset for potential algorithmic bias?
3. [ ] Can I translate regulatory requirements (e.g., EU AI Act, GDPR) into technical constraints for developers?
4. [ ] Can I draft a comprehensive Enterprise AI Policy covering acceptable use and risk mitigation?
5. [ ] Do I understand adversarial risks like prompt injection and how to document requirements to prevent them?

### Domain 4: Conceptual AI Architecture
1. [ ] Can I visually map a multi-agent orchestration workflow (e.g., using Mermaid.js or similar tools)?
2. [ ] Do I know when to recommend a Small Language Model (SLM) over a Large Language Model (LLM) based on use case constraints (latency, cost, privacy)?
3. [ ] Can I design an agentic "router" architecture that routes queries to specialized models based on intent?
4. [ ] Do I understand the concept of Agentic Memory (short-term vs. long-term) and its business implications?
5. [ ] Can I architect a system that transitions gracefully from an AI failure back to human support (graceful degradation)?

### Domain 5: Implementation & Change Management
1. [ ] Can I map and manage stakeholders through the AI Adoption Curve, addressing specific "AI Anxiety"?
2. [ ] Have I designed a human-AI collaboration process flow, redefining job roles rather than simply replacing them?
3. [ ] Can I design continuous feedback loops that capture human corrections to improve model accuracy over time?
4. [ ] Do I know how to transition an organization from legacy rule-based RPA to dynamic Agentic workflows?
5. [ ] Can I build an upskilling roadmap for an organization adopting widespread AI tooling?
- **Current Score: __ / 25**

### Domain 6: ROI & Success Measurement
1. [ ] Can I build a comprehensive Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) model for a multi-agent platform, factoring in compute, maintenance, and data pipeline costs?
2. [ ] Do I know how to measure and quantify the "intangible" value of AI (e.g., improved decision quality, reduced cognitive load)?
3. [ ] Can I define accuracy, hallucination, and reliability KPIs specifically for autonomous agents?
4. [ ] Can I build a compelling C-Suite Business Case that compares the financial impact of AI adoption against the risk of inaction?
5. [ ] Can I design a post-implementation audit to verify that projected ROI is actually being realized?
