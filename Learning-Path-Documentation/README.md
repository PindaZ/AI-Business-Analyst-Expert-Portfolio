# Learning Path Documentation: The AI-Expert Business Analyst

Welcome to the official documentation for the **AI-Expert Business Analyst Learning Path**. This folder serves as your "North Star" for navigating the complex intersection of AI technology and enterprise strategy.

## 🌟 Our Mission
To bridge the gap between AI engineering and business value. We aim to equip Business Analysts (BAs) with the strategic, ethical, and technical fluency required to lead AI initiatives in 2026 and beyond.

## 🧑‍💼 The 2026 AI-Expert BA Persona
The modern BA is no longer just a requirements gatherer; they are an **AI Orchestrator**. Key traits include:
- **Strategic Alignment**: Linking AI capabilities to high-level business goals (ROI, ESG, Market Share).
- **Data Fluency**: Understanding the nuances of unstructured data, RAG, and data moats.
- **Ethical Leadership**: Proactively managing bias, transparency, and human-in-the-loop (HITL) governance.
- **Architectural Intuition**: Designing agentic workflows and model-tiering strategies.

## 🗺️ How to Use This Path
1.  **Start with the [Curriculum Map](./01-Curriculum-Map.md)**: Understand the 60-topic journey and where you currently stand.
2.  **Follow the Phases**: The curriculum is designed to be followed sequentially from "Strategy Discovery" to "ROI Measurement."
3.  **Engage with the [Resources](./02-Learning-Resources.md)**: Use our curated list of external courses and whitepapers to deepen your knowledge.
4.  **Track Your Progress**: Use the **[Self-Assessment Tool](./05-Self-Assessment-Tool.md)** at the end of each phase.
5.  **Achieve [Milestones](./03-Milestones-Certifications.md)**: Aim for the suggested industry certifications to validate your expertise.
