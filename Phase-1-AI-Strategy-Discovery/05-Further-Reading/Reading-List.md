# Phase 1 Reading List: Strategic AI Foundations

To master the concepts in **Phase 1: AI Strategy & Value Discovery**, we recommend the following curated high-impact resources.

## 🏛️ Foundational Strategy
- **"Competing in the Age of AI"** (Marco Iansiti & Karim R. Lakhani): The definitive guide on how AI removes traditional scale constraints.
- **"Prediction Machines"** (Agrawal, Gans, Goldfarb): Understand the "Economics of Intelligence" and how lowering the cost of prediction changes everything.

## 📰 Essential Articles & Whitepapers
- **[The Economic Potential of Generative AI](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-digital/our-insights/the-economic-potential-of-generative-ai-the-next-productivity-frontier)** (McKinsey): A deep dive into the $4.4 trillion opportunity.
- **[How Generative AI Will Transform Business Strategy](https://www.bcg.com/publications/2023/generative-ai-transform-business-strategy)** (BCG): A framework for the "Value Flip" in the enterprise.

## 🎧 Podcasts for the Commute
- **[The A16Z Podcast: AI Revolution](https://a16z.com/podcasts/)**: High-level venture capital perspective on where the "Value Moats" are being built.
- **[Hard Fork (NYT)](https://www.nytimes.com/column/hard-fork)**: Great for keeping up with the rapid weekly changes in the AI landscape.

## 🛠️ Tooling for Strategy BAs
- **[Miro AI Strategy Templates](https://miro.com/templates/ai-strategy/)**: Practical boards for running the workshops described in our labs.
- **[OpenAI Tokenizer](https://platform.openai.com/tokenizer)**: Use this to visualize how your text is broken down into tokens for cost estimation.

---
*Note: Always prioritize reading the original source papers of new models (e.g., "Attention is All You Need") to understand the technical constraints that drive business strategy.*
