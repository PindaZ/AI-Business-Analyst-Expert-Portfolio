# AI Opportunity Matrix

The AI Opportunity Matrix is a strategic framework used by Business Analysts to prioritize AI initiatives based on their **Business Impact** and **Technical Feasibility**.

## The Four Quadrants

1.  **Low-Hanging Fruit (High Impact, High Feasibility)**:
    *   **Focus**: Immediate ROI and quick wins.
    *   **Examples**: Automating repetitive data entry, sentiment analysis for customer support, basic chatbots for internal FAQs.
2.  **Strategic Bets (High Impact, Low Feasibility)**:
    *   **Focus**: Long-term competitive advantage and "moat" building.
    *   **Examples**: Predictive maintenance for global supply chains, personalized medicine, autonomous logistics.
3.  **Experimental Pilots (Low Impact, High Feasibility)**:
    *   **Focus**: Learning and building AI literacy within the organization.
    *   **Examples**: AI-assisted email drafting, internal search optimization.
4.  **Avoid/Defer (Low Impact, Low Feasibility)**:
    *   **Focus**: Resource conservation.
    *   **Characteristics**: High R&D cost with minimal business value.

## Assessment Criteria

### Business Impact
- Revenue Generation
- Cost Reduction
- Risk Mitigation
- Customer Experience Enhancement

### Technical Feasibility
- Data Availability & Quality
- Model Readiness (Off-the-shelf vs. Custom)
- Integration Complexity
- Regulatory/Ethical Constraints
