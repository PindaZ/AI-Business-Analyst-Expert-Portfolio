# AI-ROI-Formula

The AI-ROI-Formula is a strategic framework for measuring the financial impact of AI initiatives on the business.

## The Five Components of AI ROI

1.  **Revenue Growth**:
    *   **Goal**: Increasing revenue through AI-driven products or services.
    *   **BA Metric**: Revenue growth from AI-enhanced cross-selling or personalized marketing.
2.  **Cost Reduction**:
    *   **Goal**: Reducing operational costs through AI automation.
    *   **BA Metric**: Cost reduction from AI-automated customer support or supply chain optimization.
3.  **Risk Mitigation**:
    *   **Goal**: Reducing the risk of financial or reputational harm through AI-driven fraud detection or compliance monitoring.
    *   **BA Metric**: Savings from prevented fraud or reduced compliance penalties.
4.  **Customer Experience Enhancement**:
    *   **Goal**: Improving customer satisfaction and loyalty through AI-driven personalization and support.
    *   **BA Metric**: Customer retention rate improvement and Net Promoter Score (NPS) growth.
5.  **Employee Productivity**:
    *   **Goal**: Increasing employee productivity through AI-assisted task completion.
    *   **BA Metric**: Time saved per task and employee satisfaction improvement.

## The AI ROI Formula (Conceptual)

### Total Benefit (TB)
*   **TB = Revenue Growth + Cost Reduction + Risk Mitigation + CX Enhancement + Productivity Gains**

### Total Cost (TC)
*   **TC = Development Cost + Token Cost + Maintenance Cost + Ethical/Compliance Risk Cost**

### ROI Calculation
*   **ROI (%) = ( (TB - TC) / TC ) * 100**

## BA Assessment Checklist

*   **Financial Impact**: What is the projected financial impact of this AI initiative?
*   **Time to ROI**: How long will it take for the business to see a positive ROI?
*   **Risk vs. Reward**: Are the potential risks associated with this AI project worth the reward?
