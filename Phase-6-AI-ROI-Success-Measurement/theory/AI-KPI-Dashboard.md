# AI-KPI-Dashboard

The AI-KPI-Dashboard is a strategic framework for tracking the performance and impact of AI systems in an enterprise.

## The Three Levels of AI Metrics

1.  **Model Performance (Technical)**:
    *   **Goal**: Measuring the accuracy and reliability of the AI model.
    *   **BA Metric**: Precision, recall, F1-score, and hallucination rate.
2.  **Operational Performance (Process)**:
    *   **Goal**: Measuring the efficiency and scalability of the AI-driven process.
    *   **BA Metric**: Latency, cost per query, and task completion time.
3.  **Business Impact (Strategic)**:
    *   **Goal**: Measuring the real-world impact of the AI initiative on the business.
    *   **BA Metric**: Revenue growth, cost reduction, and customer satisfaction (NPS).

## The AI KPI Dashboard Framework

### 1. The Real-Time Model Monitor
*   **Metric 1**: Accuracy (Are model predictions correct?).
*   **Metric 2**: Hallucination Rate (How often does the model generate false information?).
*   **Metric 3**: Latency (How long does the model take to generate a response?).

### 2. The Operational Efficiency Tracker
*   **Metric 1**: Cost per Query (What is the cost of running the model?).
*   **Metric 2**: Task Completion Rate (How many tasks are successfully completed by AI?).
*   **Metric 3**: Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Rate (How many AI decisions require human review?).

### 3. The Business Value Dashboard
*   **Metric 1**: Projected vs. Actual ROI (Is the AI project meeting its financial goals?).
*   **Metric 2**: Customer Satisfaction (Is AI improving the customer experience?).
*   **Metric 3**: Employee Productivity (Is AI saving employees time and effort?).

## BA Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E)

- **Step 1: Metric Selection**: Identifying the most relevant AI KPIs for the business.
- **Step 2: Data Collection & Tracking**: Collecting and tracking AI metrics over time.
- **Step 3: Insights & Action**: Analyzing AI KPIs and taking action to improve performance and impact.
