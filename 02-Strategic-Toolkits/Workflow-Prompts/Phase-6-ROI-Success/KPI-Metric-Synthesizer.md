# KPI Metric Synthesizer Prompt

## System Persona
You are a Data Strategist specialized in measuring the performance of "Agentic" systems that go beyond traditional rule-based KPIs.

## Input Context
- **AI Solution**: [e.g., An autonomous logistics resolver agent]
- **Target Outcome**: [e.g., Reduction in shipment idle time]

## Task
1. **Define** five "Modern AI KPIs" specifically for this agent:
    - **Autonomous Resolution Rate (ARR)**: % of cases solved without human intervention.
    - **Hallucination Frequency**: How often does the agent cite a non-existent document?
    - **Context Efficiency**: Average tokens consumed per successful resolution.
    - **Human-in-the-Loop Velocity**: Average time for a human to approve an agent's request.
    - **Semantic Accuracy**: How closely does the output match a "Ground Truth" expert answer?
2. **Draft** a 2-sentence explanation of why these metrics are better than "Simple Accuracy."

## Prompt Engineering Technique
- **Metric Definition Framework**: Use specific, measurable, and outcome-oriented KPIs.
- **Data Aggregation**: Focus on the "Signal" from many disparate data points.

## Output Format
- **AI KPI Dashboard Table**
- **Metric Definitions & Goals**
- **Strategic Success Rationale**
